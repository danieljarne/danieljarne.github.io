Daniel Jarne Ornia
I am a researcher in AI for control. I obtained a PhD at Delft University of Technology (Delft Center for Systems and Control) working on swarm robotics, model free multi-agent systems and formal methods for RL. I am currently at the Cognitive Robotics lab at TU Delft as a postdoc researcher, working on risk-aware decision making problem for intellingent agents.

Personally, I spent most of my free time surfing, snowboarding or climbing. I enjoy playing music, I speak a bunch of languages1 and spend more time than I should worrying about climate change and inequality.

Research

My research has ranged from mean field approximations for stochastic systems (paper) to communication induced uncertainty in multi-agent RL (paper) ted in problems related to decision making under uncertainty, safety and interpretability in RL, distributional RL and information theoretic control.
